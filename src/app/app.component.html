Our Story
A little over 15 years ago, Brittany and Noran made the winding journey up the mountain to attend Appalachian State University. Unbeknownst to the two of them, they moved into their dorm rooms only one floor apart. One day, Brittany's floor decided to organize a movie night and posted flyers in the elevator inviting anyone in the dorm who wanted to come. What was the movie they chose you ask? A lovely and totally appropriate film from 2007 called SuperBad. Out of an entire 8-floor dorm building, Noran was the only person with enough gumption to attend a movie night full of strangers. There, the two of them became fast friends. Over the next decade and a half, there would continue to be numerous adventures, countless new things to learn about one another, and many more movie nights. All of you have been a part of our unforgettable journey, and we would like to thank you for celebrating this new chapter of our lives with us. <3

FAQs
What should I wear? Semi-formal attire meaning, no ties but preferably no jeans.
What are we eating? Breakfast for dinner!
Indoors or outdoors? We are planning to have everything outside but will move inside or have a tent for inclement weather.
What's the parking situation? Parking will be somewhat tight, so carpooling is encouraged. Please do not park directly to the left of the main cabin per venue rules.
Will alcoholic beverages be served? We will have 2-3 signature cocktails and beer available.
Can I bring a plus one? Please discuss additional guests with Brittany at least 3 weeks before the day of the wedding to ensure an accurate headcount for our caterer.
